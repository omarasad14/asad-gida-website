Our Story

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Passion

ASAD Gıda Sanayi was founded with a singular vision: to become Türkiye's most comprehensive coffee industry group. From sourcing the finest green beans at origin to delivering roasted perfection to consumers worldwide, we operate across every link in the coffee value chain.

As the parent company of ToptanKahveler, FrancesCoffee, and Roasted.com, we've built an ecosystem that serves B2B wholesale buyers, premium retail consumers, and the next generation of café entrepreneurs — all under one roof.

What We Do

End-to-End Coffee Solutions

From green bean sourcing to café franchise operations — we cover the entire coffee industry value chain.

Green Bean Export

Direct-origin sourcing and international export of premium green coffee beans from the world's finest growing regions.

  • Single-origin & specialty lots
  • Direct farm relationships
  • Full export documentation
  • Global shipping logistics

Wholesale Distribution

Bulk supply of green and roasted coffee beans to roasters, retailers, and hospitality businesses across Türkiye and beyond.

  • Competitive bulk pricing
  • Consistent quality supply
  • Flexible order volumes
  • Dedicated account management

Roasting Raw Materials

Complete raw material supply for roasting operations — from green beans to packaging materials and equipment sourcing.

  • Curated bean selections
  • Roast profile guidance
  • Packaging solutions
  • Equipment procurement

Café Franchising

Turn-key café franchise solutions — from concept design to daily operations — under our proven brand frameworks.

  • Proven business models
  • Location analysis & fit-out
  • Staff training programs
  • Ongoing operational support

Industry Consultancy

Expert advisory services for businesses entering or scaling within the coffee industry — strategy, sourcing, and operations.

  • Market entry strategy
  • Supply chain optimization
  • Brand development
  • Operational audits

Coffee Tech & Digital

Through Roasted.com, we build digital e-commerce platforms and tools that modernize coffee industry operations and wholesale transactions.

  • B2B ordering platforms
  • Inventory management
  • Customer engagement apps
  • Data-driven insights
